Learning System

Understanding beats engagement.

This surface helps you track what clarified, what lingered, and what still needs work as you move through Asymmetric Precision.

Why this exists

AP measures clarity, not vanity.

Most sites ask for likes, shares, stars, and engagement. AP asks a different question: did this help you understand the system more clearly? Your reflection trail is stored locally in your browser so it can help you continue learning without creating an account or sending data anywhere.

Signal

What became clearer?

When an idea lands, the system records the moment locally so you can see which surfaces improved your understanding.

Uncertainty

What still needs thought?

Complex systems deserve reflection. “Still thinking” is not failure. It is a useful signal that the idea is still integrating.

Observation

What remains unclear?

If a page does not clarify the system yet, capture the unresolved question. That observation can guide your next path through AP.

Local-first by design

No account. No backend. No engagement trap.

This reflection log stays in your browser. You can export it, clear it, or use it to decide what to explore next. AP does not need to own your learning trail for the trail to help you.
