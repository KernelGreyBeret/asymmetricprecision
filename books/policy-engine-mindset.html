The Policy Engine Mindset

Understanding systems through decision logic, incentives, and constraints.

Abstract

Every system contains a policy engine, whether formalized or not.

Sometimes it is software.
Sometimes it is culture.
Sometimes it is fear.
Sometimes it is incentives.

The Policy Engine Mindset explores how decisions are actually made inside complex systems and why understanding decision logic is the key to understanding behavior itself.

Drawing from Zero Trust Architecture, enterprise governance, operational leadership, and systems theory, Tommy Burke reframes the Policy Decision Point (PDP) as more than a cybersecurity component — it becomes a universal model for understanding control, authorization, adaptation, and human systems.

This book challenges readers to:

stop focusing only on actions and start examining the policies, incentives, and constraints that produced those actions in the first place.
