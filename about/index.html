About

Operator. Architect. Writer.

Asymmetric Precision is the public home for the writings, frameworks, books, and operational philosophy of Tommy Burke.

The work

Built from lived complexity.

This site exists to collect and publish ideas shaped by military operations, cybersecurity, systems architecture, leadership, teaching, engineering, analysis, and years of working inside organizations under pressure. The goal is simple: make complex systems easier to see, explain, defend, and improve.

Lens

Systems first

People, tools, policies, incentives, constraints, and environments all interact. The system is usually telling the truth.

Method

Operational clarity

Ideas should survive contact with real constraints, real people, real organizations, and real consequences.

Motto

Tactical compassion

Precision without humanity becomes brittle. Compassion without discipline becomes noise. The work lives between them.
