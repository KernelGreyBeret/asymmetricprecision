# The AP Oath

**Version:** 0.1  
**Status:** Draft for AP v2.0 front-door experience

---

Before you begin:

Be willing to question your assumptions.

Follow evidence where it leads.

Seek understanding before judgment.

Look for the system beneath the symptom.

Respect complexity without surrendering to confusion.

Use precision without losing humanity.

Leave every system better understood than you found it.

If that is how you are trying to think, welcome.

You are among friends.
