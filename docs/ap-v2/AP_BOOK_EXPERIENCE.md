# AP Book Experience

Version 0.11

## Principle

A book is not a product. A book is a curriculum path.

## Purpose

Books are the long-form curriculum of Asymmetric Precision. Essays introduce lenses. Frameworks become operating models. Books sequence those ideas into durable study paths.

## Reader Outcome

Every book page should make clear what the reader will understand differently after reading the book.

## Required Surfaces

- Curriculum stage
- Primary domain
- Difficulty
- Reader outcome
- Related principles
- Related essays
- Related frameworks
- Atlas connections
- Next curriculum path

## Design Rule

Do not present books as a catalog. Present them as a path through the AP school of thought.
