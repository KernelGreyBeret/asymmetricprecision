# AP v2 Launch Candidate

Version: `2.0.0-rc1`

## Principle

**A launch is not a finish line. A launch is a declared standard.**

AP v2 is not complete because the body of work is complete.

AP v2 is launch-ready when the public experience reflects the standard we intend to keep building against.

## What AP v2 Now Is

Asymmetric Precision v2 is the public home of a school of applied systems thinking.

It includes:

- A declared mission
- A public oath
- A canonical vocabulary
- A taxonomy of domains
- A design system
- A relationship-first Atlas
- Content as learning surfaces
- Essays as lenses
- Frameworks as operating models
- Books as curriculum paths
- Field Notes as observations
- Laboratories as evidence surfaces
- A learning system focused on understanding

## Launch Standard

AP v2 is ready to launch when a thoughtful first-time visitor can answer:

1. What is Asymmetric Precision?
2. Why does it exist?
3. How does it think?
4. What can I explore next?
5. Did I leave understanding something more clearly?

## Launch Candidate Scope

This release candidate does not require every essay, framework, book, or laboratory to be fully migrated.

It requires the system to be coherent enough that future content has a durable place to live.

## Decision Rule

If a change increases coherence, understanding, access, or intentionality, it may enter the launch candidate.

If a change merely adds novelty, delay it.
